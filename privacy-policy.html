Privacy Policy Effective date: July 15, 2026 Dopamine Reset ("we," "us," "our") helps people build healthier habits by replacing compulsive behaviors around social media, pornography, and food. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how you can control it. 1. Information we collect Account information: your email address and password (handled by our authentication provider, Supabase — we never see or store your raw password). Habit data you provide: the addictions you select to work on, your personal "why" statement, daily check-ins (clean/relapse status, mood, and what triggered a relapse), and computed streaks. Coach chat messages: anything you type to our AI coach, and the coach's replies, so your conversation history persists across sessions. Subscription status: whether you're on a free trial or paid subscription, managed through RevenueCat and the App Store — we do not collect or store your payment card details. Device/technical data: basic crash and error diagnostics (via Sentry) once you use the app, to help us fix bugs. This may include device type, OS version, and app version. 2. How we use your information To operate core features: tracking streaks, running daily check-ins, and powering the AI coach chat. To personalize the AI coach's responses using your selected addictions, current streaks, and stated reason for resetting. To manage your subscription and free trial. To diagnose crashes and fix bugs. To communicate with you about your account (e.g. password resets) — we do not send marketing email unless you ask us to. 3. Third parties we share data with We use a small number of infrastructure providers to run the app. We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with advertisers. Supabase — hosts our database and handles authentication. Stores your profile, check-ins, streaks, and chat history. OpenAI — processes the text you send to the AI coach (and the context needed to answer you: your addictions, streaks, and "why") in order to generate a reply. OpenAI does not receive your email address or payment information. By default, OpenAI does not use API data to train its models, and retains it for up to 30 days solely for abuse-monitoring purposes before deletion. We ask for your explicit consent to this processing when you create your account, separate from your general acceptance of these policies, because some of what you may share (for example, related to pornography-use habits) can be considered sensitive personal data in some jurisdictions. RevenueCat and Apple (App Store) — handle subscription billing and status. We receive only your entitlement status (active/inactive), never your card details. Sentry — receives crash reports and error logs to help us keep the app stable. 4. Sensitive data Because this app deals with addiction recovery — including topics like pornography use — some of what you enter (relapse triggers, check-in notes, chat messages) may be considered sensitive personal data. We treat it accordingly: it is only ever used to power your own experience in the app (streak tracking, coach responses) and is never used for advertising or sold to third parties. 5. Data retention We retain your data for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account (Settings → Delete account), we permanently delete your profile, check-ins, streaks, and chat history from our database. This action cannot be undone. 6. Your rights Access: you can review your data in-app at any time (streaks, check-in history, chat history). Deletion: delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Delete account. Correction: contact us if any of your data is inaccurate and you're unable to fix it yourself in-app. 7. Children's privacy Dopamine Reset is rated 17+ and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 17. If you believe a minor has used the app to create an account, contact us and we will delete it. 8. Security We use industry-standard practices to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and database access controls that restrict who — including us — can read or modify your data. 9. Changes to this policy We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above, and, where required, we'll notify you in-app. 10.
